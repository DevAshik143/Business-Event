Some Questions ?

What is the dress code for the event?

The recommended dress code is business casual. However, for specific events like gala dinners or networking evenings, formal attire may be required. Please check the event schedule for details.

Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?

Yes, tickets are transferable. Please contact our support team with the new attendee's information at least 48 hours before the event.

Will the event be recorded or live-streamed?

Yes, key sessions will be recorded and made available to registered participants after the event. Some parts of the event may also be live-streamed. Stay tuned for updates!

Are meals and refreshments included in the ticket price?

Yes, your ticket includes access to coffee breaks, lunch, and evening networking refreshments. Please let us know in advance if you have any dietary restrictions.
